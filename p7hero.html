Walter White

Father, Husband & Teacher

Biography

Walter Hartwell White was born on September 7, 1958. His father died of Huntington's disease early in his life and he delved deep into his studies to cope. Walter studied at Caltech proving to be a brilliant chemist, specializing in crystallography. In 1985, Walt's groundbreaking research found its way to a project that was eventually awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry. He also co-founded Gray Matter Technologies which went on to become a multi-billion dollar corporation but Walter saw little profit as he sold his share for a mere $5,000.

Why Walter White is my Hero

Walter White is the chemistry teacher at J. P. Wynne High School. I admire his vast knowledge as a chemist as well as his dedication to his students and family. As a family man, Walter cares for his son, Walter White Jr. who suffers from Cerebral Palsy. I also admire Walter's self relience. When diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, Walter worked even more hours, starting his business to pay the bills. Eventually his hard work lead to a 80 milion dollar fortune.
